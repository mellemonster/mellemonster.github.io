Global 10 Syllabus

Expectations

Classwork:

We are going to be doing many fun things in class this year. We will become historians and examine the past. To do this, we will need to work hard and play hard.

Homework:

If you do not finish classwork in time it will become homework. We will also be having quizzes that will require you to study at home.

Discussions:

We will be having discussions in class. You need to be prepared to be a good citizen in class.

Rules:

Be on time! Be respectful of Jeremy, your classmates, and yourself!

What We Are Learning?

We are learning about different places and people during world history. To get this done, we are breaking down world history into 8 Eras.

Era 1 Rise of Humans in the Universe

Era 2 Rise of Social Classes

Era 3 Rise of Great Empires & Great Inequalities

Era 4 Rise of Major Religions

Era 5 Rise of Racism & Colonialism

Era 6 Rise of Revolutions

Era 7 Rise of Global War

Era 8 Rise of a Connected World

Why Are We Learning This?

We are learning lots of history this year! The reason for all of these topics is to get you prepared for the Global Regents Exam. We will be looking at the most important events in each era to make sure you will have a solid base of knowledge for the test.
