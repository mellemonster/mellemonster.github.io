Class Constitution

Personal History

We will make personal history books about ourselves! You need to include the following

  • Paragraph biography about who you are now! What do you like/dislike.
  • Flag of your Paradigm. You will create a flag that represents how you have changed during your life.
  • Include a primary or secondary source about yourself. This could be a memory, a picture, an article, or a song. Be creative and say what you chose and why.
  • Write a brief statement about why you will go down in history.
