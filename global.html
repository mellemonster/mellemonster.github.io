Global 10

World History Through Social Class

Google Classroom

Essential Questions

  • How did people come to rule the world?
  • Why there different types of people?
  • Why do few people have so much and lots of people have so little?
  • How did racism, sexism, and other forms of hatred happen?
  • Is there a way to make the world a better place?

Objectives

  • Define and describe social classes
  • Tell the history of the world through the creation of different classes of people
  • Connect themes of history to current events

Historical Eras

  • Era 0 - Rise of the Class
  • Era 1 - Rise of Humans
  • Era 2 - Rise of "civilization"
  • Era 3 - Rise of great empires and great inequality
  • Era 4 - Rise of major religions
  • Era 5 - Rise of racism and colonialism
  • Era 6 - Rise of revolutions
  • Era 7 - Rise of global war
  • Era 8 - Rise of the connected world

Research Assignments (Transfer Tasks)

  • Choose one of the discussion questions from Eras 5-8 and write an argumentative research paper answering the question.

Class Final

  • You will take a mock regents exam at the end of the course
  • You will write a reflection at the end of the class about what they learned. They need to tie everything together in this reflection!
